General Information

The Mighty Ducks Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing Chicago's northside
neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play hockey. To be a member of MDHL, you must be between
the ages of 4 - 12 and reside a Chicago northside neighborhood. MDHL ran by o small full-time staff, and
relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
